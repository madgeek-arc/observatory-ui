Open Science Country Pages

The Open Science Country Pages offer a detailed and comprehensive view of Open Science progress and initiatives across various European countries. The Open Science Country Pages map the diverse national Research and Development (R&D) landscapes in Europe to provide a 360o view on the Open Science maturity of more than 35 countries. The Country Pages compile information about Open Science policies, key actors, initiatives, digital infrastructure and repositories as well as on training and skills employed to realise them. By selecting a country from the interactive map, you can access specific information on national policies, practices, and impacts related to Open Science

How are the data collected

The data presented in the EOSC Open Science Observatory country pages are aggregated from multiple credible sources to provide a comprehensive and accurate overview of the Open Science situation in each country. Each data source is clearly indicated both by its corresponding text and a color-coded line at the top of each tile. The four main data sources include:

Survey on National Contributions to EOSC and Open Science
OpenAIRE Graph
NOADs Country Narratives
External Data Sources (e.g. Eurostat, EOSC Portal, CoARA)
