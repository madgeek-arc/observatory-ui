Overview

The Czech Republic, which holds a dense scientific community working on different aspects of the landscape, is advancing towards the implementation of a full strategy for Open Science.Czech universities and further research and funding institutions have made various efforts to develop and adopt Open Science in all disciplines. The working groups that have been set upt study specific easpects of EOSC's implementation in the Czech Republic (EOSC-CZ) resembles the Task Forces organised by the EOSC Association.
