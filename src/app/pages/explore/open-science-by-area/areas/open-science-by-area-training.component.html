Training is essential for the successful adoption of Open Science, equipping key stakeholders with the skills to implement open practices, share research outputs, and foster global collaboration. This page offers an overview of skills and training initiatives in Europe, including policies, financial investments, and strategies from the Survey on National Contributions to EOSC and Open Science.
